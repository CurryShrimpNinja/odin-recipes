Soda-Gembira

Soda gembira (literally, happy soda) is a popular mocktail made with soda water,

coco pandan syrup, sweetened condensed milk, and crushed/shaved ice.

Ingredients

  • 330ml Chilled Soda Water or Seltzer
  • 1 tablespoon Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 tablespoon Cocopandan Syrup
  • Ice Cubes

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients into a glass.
  2. Stir and Enjoy
