RumChata White-Russian

Got RumChata and looking for a way to use it?

you’re a lover of sweet and creamy drinks, try the RumChata White Russian!

spin on the classic drink is an ideal use for the cinnamon and cream liqueur, pairing it with coffee liqueur and vodka.

It’s intensely sweet, so plan on nursing this slow sipper all evening.

Here’s how to make it!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce Kahlua
  • 1 ounce Rumchata

Steps

  1. Add the vodka, Kahlua and Rumchata to a glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir and Enjoy.
