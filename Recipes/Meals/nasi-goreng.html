Nasi-Goreng (Indonesian Fried Rice)

Ingredients

  • Chicken
    • 1 tbps Oil
    • 5 oz/150g Chicken Breast thinly sliced
    • 1 tbsp Kecap Manis (sweet soy sauce)

  • Rice
    • 1.5 tbsp oil
    • 2 garlic cloves finely chopped
    • 1 tsp red chillifinely chopped
    • 1 onion small, diced
    • 3 cups cooked white rice day old, cold
    • 2 tbso kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
    • 2 tsp shrimp paste (optional)

  • Garnishes/Side Servings (Optional)
    • 4 eggs fried to taste
    • 1 green onion sliced
    • Tomatoes and cucumbers cut into wedges/chunks
    • Fried Shallots
    • Lime Wedges
